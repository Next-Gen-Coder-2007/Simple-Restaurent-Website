What's poke

Poke is a Hawaiian dish featuring nice diced raw fish, commonly tuna or salmon, marinated in soy sauce, sesame oil, and spices. Served over rice, it's topped with ingredients like seaweed, avocado, and sesame seeds, creating a delicious blend of flavors and textures. Known for its freshness and healthiness, poke is a globally loved dish that reflects Hawaiian traditions and offers endless customization for seafood enthusiasts.
